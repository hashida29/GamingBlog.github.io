Mobile Legends Patch 1.7.66 Update: Hero Adjustments, Battlefield Adjustments and more

Developers have introduced new changes with a old Emblem system!

BY CHIRAG CHAUDHURI MARCH 12, 2023

With the onset of the newest Patch 1.7.66 Update in the Mobile Legends Advanced Server, developers at MOONTON Games have decided to test the old Emblem system once again, before reverting to the new version of the Emblem system once again.

Mobile Legends Patch 1.7.66 Update: Hero Adjustments

Esmeralda (↑)

Developers have slightly increased Esmeralda’s damage to make her more competitive than other EXP laners.

Basic Attack (↑)
  • First Damage: 80% Physical Attack >> 90% Physical Attack.
  • Second Damage: 100 +55% Magic Power >> 100 +65% Magic Power.
Skill 2 (↑)
  • Cooldown: 5.5-3.5s >> 4.5-3.5s.

Freya (↑)

Developers have optimized Freya’s gameplay. She can move through walls with Leap of Faith more quickly and the third hit of Spirit Combo now has less foreswing time, but also a shorter airborne duration. Developers have also adjusted Spirit Combo’s growth rate to be more reasonable.

Skill 1 (↑)
  • Moves through obstacles more easily.
Skill 2 (↑)
Reduced the third hit’s foreswing time.
  • Airborne Duration: 0.5s >> 0.4s.
  • Base Damage: 50-100 (+50% Extra Physical Attack) >> 80-230 (+90% Extra Physical Attack).
  • Final Damage: 100-200 (+100% Extra Physical Attack) >> 121-276 (+108% Extra Physical Attack).
  • Shield: 100-150 (+409 Extra Physical Attack) >> 120-260 (+70% Extra Physical Attack).

Paquito (↑)

Developers have slightly buffed Paquito’s durability in the mid and late game to fit his role as a fighter.

Skill 1 (↑)
  • Shield: 150-500 +110% Physical Attack >> 150-550 +120% Physical Attack.
