Mobile Legends Patch 1.7.64 Update: Hero Adjustments, Battlefield Adjustments and more

BY CHIRAG CHAUDHURI MARCH 7, 2023

After introducing a new hero Novaria in the last Patch 1.7.62 Update in the Mobile Legends Advanced Server, Patch 1.7.64 brings more changes to other heroes, emblem system changes and more. Some of the emblems of players have been reverted back to old stats so that players can test other relevant features. In case a player in the match has old emblems, all the players in the game will be shifted back to old emblems.

This, along with more balance adjustments to heroes and equipment forms the basis of the newest Patch 1.7.64 Update in the Mobile Legends Advanced Server. Let us break down the patch in detail.

Mobile Legends Patch 1.7.64 Update: Hero Adjustments

Novaria (Buffed)

Developers at MOONTON Games have fixed some issues with Novaria and optimized her Skill 2.

Skill 1 (↑)
  • Slightly increased her lane-clearing speed in the mid and late game.
Skill 2 (↑)
  • Increased the projectile’s speed, and added foreswing animation and warning effects.
  • Fixed an issue where the Movement Speed increase was not always triggering correctly.

Alpha (Buffed)

Experimental Change: Optimized Alpha’s gameplay and buffed Beta’s effectiveness.

Passive
  • Revamped: Beta will lock on an enemy after hitting them twice. When Alpha attacks a locked-on target, Beta will follow up with a laser attack that deals damage and slows briefly.
  • Beta’s Laser Damage: 80 (+50% Total Physical Attack) True Damage.
Skill 1 (↑)
  • Greatly reduced the time it takes for Beta to attack.
  • Base Damage: 200-300 (+90% Extra Physical Attack) >> 100-200 (+90% Extra Physical Attack).
Skill 2 (↑)
  • Strafe Damage: 125-175 (+30% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage >> 60-120 (+30% Extra Physical Attack) True Damage.
Ultimate (~)
  • Leap Damage: 50-100 (+20% Extra Physical Attack) >> 0.
  • Landing Damage: 200-400 (+80% Extra Physical Attack) >> 250-500 (+100% Extra Physical Attack).
  • Beta’s Damage: 25-35 (+60% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage >> 15-30 (+30% Extra Physical Attack) True Damage.

Joy (Buffed)

Apart from Skill 2’s charges and the adjustment to Joy’s passive, developers have reverted all other adjustments made in the last version.

Passive (↑)
  • CD: 6s >> 4s
Ultimate (↑)
  • Ultimate hits: 6 >> 8.
  • Minimum damage after decay: 40% >> 20%.
  • Damage increase after hitting on the beat: 33% >> 40% (same max damage as the Official Server).
  • Magic Lifesteal gained after hitting all beats: 30% >> 40%.

Lesley (Buffed)

Developers have slightly buffed Lesley’s Skill 1 because the meta has been favouring Attack Speed Marksman heroes.

Skill 1 (↑)
  • Cooldown: 5-3s >> 4-2.5s.
