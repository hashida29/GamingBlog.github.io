Mobile Legends March 2023 Starlight Pass: New skins, emotes and more

Valentina to get a brand new skin!

BY DADA JOSEPH-JOHN FEBRUARY 26, 2023

The new Month, March 2023 is almost here and is featuring the debut of a new Starlight skin for mage hero, Valentina. Valentina is going to be the Lucky Mage-type hero that would be getting a Starlight Pass Event exclusive skin for March 2023. To this end, Moonton Games has officially posted a leak on their social media platforms as to what to expect for this upcoming March Pass and the likely rewards to get. Let’s check out the new skins, emotes, and many more exciting rewards in Mobile Legends that can be gotten out via the March 2023 Starlight Pass.

Mobile Legends March 2023 Starlight Pass: New Skins

New Starlight Skin

The March 2023 Starlight skin will be the Cyber Agent skin and the lucky hero to be possessing this skin will be Valentina, our amazing Copy-Ultimate Mage role hero. This skin has cool effects and makes Valentina feel like an experienced Cyber agent who’s good at technological hacks and copies.

Ever since the release of the newly revamped starlight pass, players should not expect to choose from a series of previous starlight skins in this newly revamped starlight pass. So the main skin this starlight pass is featuring is Valentina’s Cyber Agent skin

Revamped Starlight First Purchase Rewards

Apart from getting the Starlight Pass Rewards, Moonton has nerfed the price for the purchase of the Starlight Pass, and now players can get the new Starlight Pass debuting for March 2023 for as low as 300 Diamonds.

This Starlight First Time Purchase reward is meant For those players who have not bought the starlight pass ever since it has been revamped (Since February 2023). Players on buying the new March 2023 starlight pass would be given a random Summer skin Chest which will grant to players on opening any of the following skins:

  • Hayabusa Summer skin
  • Chang'e Summer skin
  • Nana Summer skin

Also, for the upcoming Starlight Shop revamp, players who have formerly bought the starlight pass before February 2023 and are just buying Starlight pass now will receive a permanent Avatar Border reward.

Mobile Legends March 2023 Starlight Pass: Other Rewards

Players who buy the new Starlight Pass would also be able to get amazing rewards like:

Starlight Exclusive Painted Skin

Players would be able to get an Exclusive Valentina, Cyber Agent painted skin after buying the Starlight Pass.

Starlight Exclusive Trail Effect

This is a newly added reward that grants players to get a new starlight exclusive Trail effect to further make the game much more glamouring for the players.

Starlight Sacred Statue

Players would be getting a Valentina, Cyber Agent exclusive Sacred Statue on buying the Starlight Pass.

Other new rewards for the Starlight Pass

The March 2023 starlight pass would also grant unique rewards like:

  • An Exclusive Starlight Killing Notification
  • Exclusive Starlight Graffiti
  • Exclusive Starlight Chat Bubble
  • Exclusive Starlight Avatar Border.

Starlight Member Benefits

Other than the above-mentioned rewards, there are a lot more privileges to being a Starlight member.
