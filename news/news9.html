Mobile Legends x Jujutsu Kaisen collab event Guide and Tips

Join the event to get amazing rewards!

BY DADA JOSEPH-JOHN FEBRUARY 23, 2023

The Mobile Legends collaboration event with popular anime, Jujutsu Kaisen is out and live in-game on the original server. The event would feature 3 different collaboration skins, as well as so many events exclusive hero voice chat, emotes, recalls, elimination effects, and much more. The lucky heroes to possess these new skins are the amazing Forsaken Light Heroes- Yin as “Itadori Yuji”, Melissa as “Nobara Kugisaki”, Xavier as “Gojo Satoru” and Julian as “Megumi Fushiguro”. Here is a detailed guide on how to get the Jujutsu Kaisen event-exclusive skins and resources in Mobile Legends.

Legends Jujutsu Kaisen skins event: Tasks and Rewards

As the event is live in-game, players can now make draws in the Jujutsu Kaisen collaboration skins event. By making draws, players would obtain Jujutsu School Crest which can be used to exchange for available Jujutsu Kaisen skins, as well as some other amazing exclusive rewards in the Jujutsu Kaisen event prize pool. The draws in the event could also be done with a ‘Cursed Charm token‘. Cursed Charm tokens can be obtained for free when players pre-register for the event before the 25th of February 2023.

They would only be able to collect the free pre-registered tokens when they log in and claim it from 25th February 2023. Cursed Charms can also be obtained by completing tasks available in the recharge bonus tokens event that would be available from the 25th of February 2023 till the 28th of February 2023.

A second batch bonus token event would also be available later in the month of March 2023. Also, players can get up to 5 free Bonus tokens from completing tasks in the bonus tokens event. Players should hence note that every first draw on a daily basis is 50% off, hence making it much easier for players to make more draws.

Mobile Legends Jujutsu Kaisen skins event: Required number of Sigil of Exorcism

All the Jujutsu Kaisen skins can be exchanged by using claimed Sigil of Exorcism tokens. Also, other exclusive resources could also be gotten via this item redeem method. Below are the amounts of Sigil of Exorcism tokens required for each item.

  • YIN “Itadori Yuji”, skin: 1200 Jujutsu School Crest
  • MELISSA “Nobara Kugisaki” skin: 1200 Jujutsu School Crest
  • XAVIER “Gojo Satoru” Skin: 1200 Jujutsu School Crest
  • JULIAN “Megumi Fushiguro” skin: 1200 Jujutsu School Crest
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Recall: 300 Jujutsu School Crest
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Spawn effect: 150 Jujutsu School Crest
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Elimination Effect: 150 Jujutsu School Crest
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Killing Notification: 225 Jujutsu School Crest
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Avatar Border: 100 Jujutsu School Crest
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Emotes (Itadori Yuji, Sukuna and Gojo Satoru): 32 Jujutsu School Crest
  • Exclusive Jujutsu kaisen’s Itadori Yuji, Sukuna, Megumi, Gojo, and Nobara quick chat voice command bundles: 10 Jujutsu School Crest

Mobile Legends Jujutsu Kaisen skins event: Possible drops from the draws

Players are to note that on the first 10 draws, a permanent event exclusive reward is guaranteed. It should hence be noted that it can’t be a Jujutsu Kaisen skin as the chances of getting a Jujutsu Kaisen skin from the draws are very low. Some of the exclusive drops from making some draws in the Jujutsu Kaisen event are as follows:

  • YIN Itadori Yuji skin
  • Melissa Nobara Kugisaki skin
  • Xavier Gojo Satoru skin
  • Julian Megumi Fushiguro skin
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Spawn effect
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Recall effect
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Elimination Effect
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Killing Notification
  • Exclusive Jujutsu Kaisen Avatar Border
  • Itadori Yuji’s ‘Delighted emote
  • Sukuna’s ‘Let’s Fight emote.
  • Gojo’s ‘Nice Job’s emote.
  • 20 Jujutsu School Crest
  • 15 Jujutsu School Crest
  • 12 Jujutsu School Crest
  • 10 Jujutsu School Crest
  • 8 Jujutsu School Crest
  • 5 Jujutsu School Crest

Mobile Legends Jujutsu Kaisen event: Exclusive features

Players accumulating 3600 Sigil of Exorcism can redeem an event exclusive skin thrice. Also, players can gift the skins two times to their in-game friends. Players can also participate in a Jujutsu Kaisen training event that will grant players free Cursed charm tokens as well as a Forsaken Light Hero Choice Chest for players who participate in the training events.

The event tasks are simple, as it includes logging in to the game daily as well as completing a specified number of Classic/Ranked/Brawl matches as well as Winning them too. Sharing the training page will also grant extra Cursed energy needed to raise one’s progress in the series of prize pools.
